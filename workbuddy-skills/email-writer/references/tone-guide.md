# 邮件语气风格参考 — 外企商务谦逊风（精简版）

基于 Carol (HR HEAD) 的邮件风格提炼，作为生成邮件时的语气模板。

## 核心调性

- **温暖但专业** — 拉近距离但不失正式感
- **谦逊有礼（humble & appreciative）** — 先感谢、再提需求
- **支持赋能型** — "My role is to empower and support you"
- **开放协作** — 保持开放姿态但不过度啰嗦

## 常用句式参考

### 开头（直接进入正题，不要套话问候）
- "I wanted to provide a brief update on..."
- "I'd like to share an update following..."
- "As part of our ongoing efforts, I'd like to..."
- "To keep you informed, here are the key developments..."

### 感谢与肯定
- "I want to take a moment to sincerely thank you for..."
- "I truly appreciate your support on..."
- "Thank you for your continued trust and collaboration."

### 委婉提出请求/建议
- "I'd like to suggest that we..."
- "I was hoping we could..."
- "Would it be possible to..."
- "I'd love to get your thoughts on..."

### 汇报进度
- "Please find below a summary of recent progress on..."
- "Based on our discussion, here's an update on..."

### 赋能/支持
- "My role is to empower and support you in..."
- "Your insights and ownership are invaluable."

### 简洁收尾（公司惯用语）
- "Please don't hesitate to let me know if any further suggestions. Thanks."

### 署名
- "Regards,"
- "Warm regards,"
- "Best regards,"

## 段落结构原则

1. **第一段：直接点明邮件目的** — 不要 "I hope this message finds you well"
2. **中间段：核心内容** — 按逻辑分段，每段一个主题
3. **最后一段：简洁收尾** — 一两句话，不要重复啰嗦

## 注意事项

- 开头不要使用 "I hope this message finds you well" / "I hope you're having a good week" 等套话
- 结尾不要使用 "I'm happy to make changes as needed" 等冗长表达
- 避免过于生硬或命令式语气
- 多使用 "I'd like to", "I was hoping", "Would it be possible" 等委婉表达
- 适时表达感谢和欣赏
- 使用 "we" 而非 "you" 来建立共同立场
- 英文用词：prefer 偏正式但不晦涩的商务词汇
- 如果涉及负面信息，用 "I wanted to share..." 或 "There's no expectation..." 等软化表达
